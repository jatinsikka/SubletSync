The problem

Why every other option fails you

Facebook groups, Craigslist, GroupMe. They were never built for subleasing. Here's what you're dealing with every time.

👻

Zero trust signals

No verification, no history, no accountability. You have no idea if the person you're Venmoing a deposit to is even real. Scams are rampant and expected.

📅

Vague availability

"Available this summer" doesn't work when your UT internship starts June 3rd and ends August 12th. Date-exact search simply doesn't exist anywhere else.

💸

Venmo and prayer

Sending $1,500 to a stranger via Venmo for a deposit is considered normal. Ghosted landlords, lost deposits, and zero recourse are the predictable outcome.

📄

No legal protection

A screenshot of a text thread is most people's "sublease agreement." When things go sideways — and they do — you have nothing to stand on.
